In Blackjack you play against the dealer. In the beginning of each round you start by betting. Then you will receive two cards, as well as the dealer. But, you only see one card of the dealer until the dealerâ€™s turn. The point of Blackjack is to get 21 with your cards. However, you do not want to have over 21. The cards in Blackjack have the value of its number. Except Ace which can have two values, one and eleven. Jack, Queen and King have the value of ten. If both you and the dealer have a value of under 21, the one who wins is the one closest to 21. However, if the value is the same the dealer wins. When you lose against the dealer you lost money that you betted. If you win you will receive twice the value of your bet. However, if you have 21 in the start it is called Blackjack and then your receive three times the value of your bet and new round is started.
